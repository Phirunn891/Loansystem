Term Installment Payment

Grouped installment is a loan repayment method where the borrower pays interest every month, but the loan principal is paid in groups for example, every 3 months or 6 months. This method helps reduce monthly payment pressure and makes it easier for borrowers to manage their finances while still keeping the loan on schedule.
